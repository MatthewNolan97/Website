Our process

Once you have dropped off your shoes, they are scrubbed down with various 5-star shoe cleaning products and brushes. Once all the dirt is removed, they are wiped off with a wet cloth. (Service ends here if the basic service is purchased)

Then the finishing touches begin; the shoes are sprayed inside with a secret odour removing spray, placed on a shoe rack upside down with airflow to speed up the process. This is repeated 3 times. Lastly but not least; the shoe is then coated with a protective product specific for the shoe's fabric.
